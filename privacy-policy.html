Privacy Policy for Fit Souls: Game Workout

Last updated: December 21, 2025

Overview

Fit Souls: Game Workout is a workout companion app designed to provide users with physical exercises based on their gameplay experience. We do not collect, store, or process any personal information, media files, or other user content on our servers.

All data you create in Fit Souls: Game Workout remains on your device and under your sole control.

Data Collection

Fit Souls: Game Workout does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data, images, videos, files, or other information. We do not use analytics, tracking, or third-party advertising SDKs.

Data Storage

The Fit Souls: Game Workout allows users to input exercise-related information (such as the exercise information or the number of repetitions). This information is stored locally on your device only and is never sent to external servers.

You maintain full control over all data on your device.

Deleting the App will permanently delete all locally stored exercise data.

Data Security

You are responsible for managing and securing your device and any exercise information stored locally in the App by your device’s own security features.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. When changes occur, the updated version will reflect a new “Last updated” date and change will be reflected at the application page in the ‘Change Log’ section of the store. Continued use of the App means you accept any modifications.

User Responsibility

Fit Souls: Game Workout suggests exercises based on game events, but you are solely responsible for assessing and managing your own physical limits.

By using the App, you agree that:

  • You determine what level of physical activity is safe and appropriate for you.
  • You should adjust or stop any exercise that causes discomfort, pain, or health concerns.
  • You should consult a qualified medical professional before beginning any new fitness activity, especially if you have health conditions, injuries, or uncertainties about your physical capacity.
  • The developer is not liable for any injuries, health issues, or consequences resulting from performing exercises suggested by the App.
  • All exercises are performed at your own risk.
