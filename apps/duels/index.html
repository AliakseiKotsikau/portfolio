Duels - Party Word Game

Duels is a fast-paced party game that turns any hangout into a battle of wits. Players take turns naming things that match a topic — movies, songs, game characters, anime, food and more — before the timer runs out. Can't answer? You lose a life. Last one standing wins!

Themed Sections

  • Cinema — actors, characters, directors, iconic roles & genres
  • Games — legendary franchises, characters, worlds & consoles
  • Music — bands, albums, songs & sing-along challenges
  • General Knowledge — geography, history, science & everyday life
  • TV Series — sitcoms, dramas, thrillers, fantasy & sci-fi
  • Anime — Naruto, One Piece, Attack on Titan & beyond
  • Franchises — Harry Potter, Star Wars, The Witcher, LOTR & more
  • and others!

Key Features

  • Perfect for parties, road trips & game nights
  • 2+ players — the more, the merrier
  • Hundreds of topics keep every round fresh
  • No internet needed — play anywhere
