Privacy Policy for Duels - Party Word Game

Last updated: March 31, 2026

Overview

Duels - Party Word Game is a party game where players challenge each other by naming things that match a given topic. We do not collect, store, or process any personal information or other user content on our servers.

All data you create in Duels remains on your device and under your sole control.

Data Collection

Duels does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data, usage data, or other information. We do not use analytics, tracking, or third-party advertising SDKs.

Data Storage

Any game-related data (such as settings or player names) is stored locally on your device only and is never sent to external servers.

You maintain full control over all data on your device.

Deleting the App will permanently delete all locally stored data.

Data Security

You are responsible for managing and securing your device and any data stored locally in the App by your device's own security features.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. When changes occur, the updated version will reflect a new "Last updated" date and the change will be reflected at the application page in the 'Change Log' section of the store. Continued use of the App means you accept any modifications.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our contact form.
